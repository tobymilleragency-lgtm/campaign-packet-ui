# Relax Remodel Consulting — Bathroom Advisory Campaign 001

## Business
Relax Remodel Consulting helps homeowners think through remodel decisions before they hire a contractor.

## Positioning
Relax Remodel is the homeowner advisor, not the contractor.

## Offer
Free project review / honest advice before you hire.

Do not use contractor-estimate language. The promise is advisory clarity, scope sanity, budget awareness, contractor-question prep, and risk reduction before the homeowner commits.

## Audience
Homeowners considering a bathroom remodel who feel unsure about budget, scope, contractor bids, material choices, or whether they are being sold more than they need.

## Market
Southeast Kansas / Northeast Oklahoma.

## Campaign goal
Drive qualified homeowners to request a free project review.

## Proof and trust points
- Independent advisory help before hiring.
- Plain-language review of project scope and questions to ask contractors.
- Helps homeowners avoid rushed decisions and unclear bids.
- No pressure to hire a contractor through the campaign.

## Primary conversion
Request a free project review.
