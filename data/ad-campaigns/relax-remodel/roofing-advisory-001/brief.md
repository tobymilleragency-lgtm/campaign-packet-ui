# Relax Remodel Consulting — roofing advisory campaign 001

## Goal
Drive homeowners with roofing projects to Relax Remodel Consulting, the veteran-owned homeowner advisor who helps them understand the project before they hire.

## Positioning
Relax Remodel is the advisor, not the contractor. The hook competitors cannot run is simple: honest advice before you hire.

Relax helps the homeowner understand roof damage, repair-vs-replace decisions, fair cost range, contractor questions, and the handoff to a vetted local contractor when the homeowner is ready.

## Offer
Free project review / honest advice before you hire.

## Primary actions
- Fill out the landing-page form.
- Tap to call Grace at 620-448-8604.

## Service area
Southeast Kansas, Northeast Oklahoma, and Southwest Missouri. Use Oswego, Parsons, and GBP service-area towns as geo anchors.

## Phase 1 scope
- 3 to 5 image ad variants. No launch video.
- One focused homeowner landing page with form and tap-to-call.
- Form POST to Relax Remodel GHL webhook.
- Confirmation / thank-you path.
- Tracking proof: GHL tracking script plus GA4 and Pixel slots.
- Iterate from real leads.

## Not in phase 1
- Generated video.
- Fake before/after claims.
- MCP or deep GHL integration.
- Switching GBP tooling.

## Creative rule
Real project photos can be enhanced, cropped, and color corrected as real work when they are actually real project photos. AI-generated images are for lifestyle, aspiration, and concept creative only. Never present AI imagery as a completed client job.
